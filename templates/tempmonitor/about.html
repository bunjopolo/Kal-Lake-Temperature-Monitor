About This Project

Welcome

Kalamalka Lake, Coldstream BC, Canada

Welcome to the Kal Lake Temperature website! There is no public temperature data that I'm aware of for Kalamalka Lake, so I created this site to provide that information for cold water swimmers and anyone else interested in tracking the lake's temperature.

The charts on the dashboard display temperature trends and monthly averages starting from August 2023.

How It Works

The temperature data is collected from a custom-built remote sensor using a Particle Boron cellular development board. The sensor records water temperatures twice daily at the north end of the lake and transmits the data to our servers via cellular connection.

The data is then processed and displayed on the dashboard, allowing you to view current conditions as well as historical trends over time.
